Hiresky â€” Automated Job Board Monitor

Hiresky is an automation tool that monitors company career boards and sends real-time alerts for new job postings. It integrates with multiple ATS APIs, filters jobs by role and location, and notifies users through Slack. The system runs automatically every morning via macOS scheduling.

  • Multi-ATS integration: Fetches live postings from Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby APIs
  • Configurable filters: YAML-driven role, company, and location selectors
  • State management: Tracks seen jobs in state.json to avoid duplicates
  • Automated notifications: Sends formatted job postings into Slack via webhooks
  • Daily scheduling: LaunchAgents + pmset wake-up for 10:00 AM runs
  • Data logging: Saves results into CSV (matches.csv) and logs for auditing

Impact: Eliminated manual checks across 50+ career sites, ensured same-day awareness of new postings, and created a reusable framework for tracking 100+ SaaS/tech companies.
